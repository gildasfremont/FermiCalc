Feature Prioritization Calculator

Based on Fermi estimation - a problem-solving method that emphasizes educated guesses and rough calculations to arrive at quick, order-of-magnitude estimates.

This tool is inspired by Jason Cohen's 'Fermi ROI: Fixing the ROI rubric' article, which introduced a systematic approach to making ROI calculations more reliable and meaningful by applying Fermi estimation principles to both impact and effort calculations.
